WHY CHOOSE GOLDCREST?

Rest assured, we are FEE-FREE

Our services are completely free of charge. We are compensated by a commission from the insurance company upon successful placement of your policy. This has always delivered a Win-Win result for us all as our focus remains on securing the best possible coverage for you, not on maximizing our own profit.

Global Expertise, Local Focus

Our team combines international, regional and national insurance expertise with a deep understanding of the Kenyan market, regulations, and trends. This facilitates tailor-made solutions that perfectly meet your specific insurance needs.

Unparalleled Choice and Competitive Rates

We partner with leading Kenyan and international insurance providers, offering you a wider range of good options to choose from. Our strong relationships allow us to negotiate the most competitive premiums on your behalf.

Dedicated Client Service

At Goldcrest, we believe in building long-term partnerships which benefit our existing and prospective clientele. Our team is committed to providing exceptional customer service, from initial consultations and risk assessments to claims processing and ongoing support.

Claims Advocacy

In the unfortunate event of a claim, you can rely on as your unwavering advocate, that prides in a track record of ensuring a smooth and efficient claims settlement process.
